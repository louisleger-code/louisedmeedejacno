LOUIS EDMEE DE JACNO

Work About Contact

Louis is a versatile creative passionate about architecture, fashion, and digital. He first gained recognition on Instagram by sharing his creations, primarily focused on design and hospitality. With his extensive experience, he has collaborated with some of the world’s most prestigious hotel groups (Airelles, Peninsula, Four Seasons, Aman), later offering his expertise through consulting.

While continuing to evolve in design and hospitality, he is now expanding his universe by returning to his passion for fashion and the art of living, putting more emphasis on his own personality. He is currently working on launching *VOLUME VOLUME*, his own digital magazine.
