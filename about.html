Work About Contact

Louis Léger, born on February 1, 1997, in Paris, is a versatile individual with multiple passions and talents. After excelling in his high school studies, Louis began studying architecture at the École nationale supérieure d'architecture de Paris. During his university years, he discovered a passion for the world of fashion and creativity. He subsequently joined AD Magazine, a renowned publication in the field of architecture and interior design, as a journalist.

However, Louis felt the need to further explore his diverse interests. He decided to venture into the fashion industry and joined Gucci’s digital team. This experience allowed him to merge his love for architectural aesthetics with fashion trends, focusing on creating innovative digital campaigns that showcased Gucci's unique style and heritage.

Throughout his experiences, Louis has also shared his passion and lifestyle on Instagram, where he creates content centered on lifestyle, fashion, design, and hospitality for his own account. His vision, enriched by his background in architecture and fashion, attracts a large audience in search of inspiration and new discoveries.

Currently, Louis is working on launching his digital magazine, "Volume Volume," which will cover these same themes, infused with his unique vision and personal artistic touch.
